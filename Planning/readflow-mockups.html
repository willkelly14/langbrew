María se despierta temprano cada sábado. Ella camina al mercado con su bolsa grande. El mercado está lleno de colores y sonidos. Los vendedores gritan los precios de sus frutas y verduras frescas.

Hoy, María quiere comprar tomates para hacer una salsa especial. Ella busca los tomates más rojos y maduros. Un vendedor amable le ofrece probar uno.

"¡Pruebe este, señora! Es el mejor del mercado," dice el vendedor con una sonrisa. María prueba el tomate y sonríe. "Perfecto," dice ella.

Después de los tomates, María camina hacia las flores. Hay ramos de todos los colores: rojos, amarillos, blancos y morados.

Getting Started Reading Flashcards AI Talk Account & Billing
Getting Started

How do I choose my target language?

During onboarding you'll pick a language and proficiency level. You can change these anytime in Settings → Language.

What's the difference between Free and Fluency?

Free gives you 10 passages per month, 1 hour of talk practice, and 1 book. Fluency unlocks 1,000 passages, 30 hours of talk, 15 book uploads, unlimited listening, and unlimited translations.

Reading

How are passages chosen for me?

langbrew selects passages based on your proficiency level and reading history, gradually increasing difficulty as you improve.

What happens when I tap a word?

Tapping a word shows its definition, pronunciation, and an example sentence. You can save it as a flashcard with one tap.

Flashcards & Vocabulary

How does spaced repetition work?

Cards you know well appear less often, while tricky cards come back sooner. The algorithm adapts to your memory patterns over time.

Can I edit or delete a flashcard?

Yes — swipe left on any card in the Vocabulary list to edit or delete it. You can also edit from the card detail view.

AI Talk Practice

What is Talk?

Talk is an AI-powered conversation partner that helps you practice speaking and listening in your target language with real-time feedback.

Are there session limits?

Free users get 1 hour of Talk practice per month. Fluency users get 30 hours per month.

Account & Billing

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Settings → Subscription to view your current plan. Billing is handled through the App Store; you can upgrade or cancel there.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Settings → Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days of the request.

Still need help?

Reach us at support@langbrew.app
